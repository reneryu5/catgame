Controls:

  • Click on the cat's face to make the cat dance.
  • Click on the cat's legs to make the cat sleep (and then click on the cat's face to wake the cat up again).
  • Click anywhere else to give the cat a fish.
  • Click "Summon Bongos" to summon bongos.
  • Click "Smol Bongo" (or use the left arrow key) to hit the smaller bongo.
  • Click "Chonky Bongo" (or use the right arrow key) to hit the bigger bongo.
  • Click "Reset" to put the cat back.

If the cat gets a good nap, it'll dream of fish.
